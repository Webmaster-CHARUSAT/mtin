Welcome to MTIN

CHARUSAT established Manikaka Topawala Institute of Nursing (formerly Charotar Institute of Nursing) in 2009 with an initial intake of 40 students. The institute has gradually expanded its offerings with B.Sc. Nursing, GNM, P.B.B.Sc. Nursing and M.Sc. Nursing in various specialties, and a Ph.D. program to foster research culture. MTIN aims to meet the growing demand of skilled nurses across hospitals, institutes and healthcare settings at state, national and global levels.

Vision

To become a premier institute in the field of Nursing Education, Practice and Research.

Mission

To prepare competent, knowledgeable and compassionate nurses with leadership qualities and a strong inclination to serve society.

Project Based Learning

Hands-on, patient-centered learning aligned with real clinical practice.

Research Facilities

Advanced labs and research culture for evidence-based nursing.

Holistic Development

Focus on ethics, leadership, communication and lifelong learning.
