Welcome to MTIN

CHARUSAT established Manikaka Topawala Institute of Nursing (formerly Charotar Institute of Nursing) in 2009 with an intake of 40 students, enhanced to 60 in 2012. GNM programme started in 2011, followed by M.Sc. Nursing in 2014 in five specialties – Medical Surgical Nursing, Child Health Nursing, Mental Health Nursing, Obstetrics & Gynecology Nursing, and Community Health Nursing.

To foster research, the Ph.D. programme commenced in 2011. The institute aims to meet the demand for skilled nurses across hospitals and institutes at state, national and global levels.

Vision

To become a premier institute in the field of Nursing Education, Practice & Research.

Mission

To prepare competent, knowledgeable and compassionate nurses with leadership qualities and an inclination to serve the society.

Project Based Learning

Application-oriented learning through projects and clinical case discussions.

State-of-the-Art Research Facilities

Modern laboratories and mentoring for UG, PG and Doctoral research.

Holistic Development

Co-curricular, extension and community activities for all-round growth.
