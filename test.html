Nursing Education @ MTIN

At CHARUSAT, we believe in holistic development of our students through strong clinical exposure, research culture and values-based learning.

Welcome to MTIN

Manikaka Topawala Institute of Nursing (formerly Charotar Institute of Nursing) was established in 2009 by CHARUSAT. The institute offers B.Sc. Nursing, GNM, Post Basic B.Sc. and M.Sc. Nursing programmes in various specialties along with Ph.D. in Nursing. MTIN aims to prepare competent, compassionate and ethical nursing professionals who can serve the healthcare needs of the society at regional, national and global levels.

Through strong hospital tie-ups, community postings and a vibrant research culture, students receive extensive clinical exposure and mentoring from experienced faculty.

Vision

To become a premier institute in the field of Nursing Education, Practice and Research.

Mission

To prepare competent, knowledgeable and compassionate nurses having leadership quality and an inclination to serve society.

Clinical Excellence

Hands-on training at multi-speciality hospitals and community health centres.

Research Culture

PG & Ph.D. programmes with focus on evidence-based nursing practice.

Holistic Campus Life

Clubs, sports and extension activities at the lush green CHARUSAT campus.
